World Map of Terrorist Attacks (2008-2017)

Country Total

What am I looking at?

This map shows terrorist attacks by country spanning 10 years (2008-2017). On the left you'll find a bar chart that shows which categories the different terrorist attacks fall into.

The countries are colored on a logarithmic scale based on the total number of terrorist attacks in that country.

Countries on the map which are colored black are missing data.

What do I do with it?

Click and drag on the map to filter the data. You'll see the bar chart and table change as you go.

To see the data for just 1 country, click the country's name in the table below
